Privacy Policy

False Start  ·  Last updated: June 1, 2026

False Start ("the App") is operated by Pavel Kozemirov. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the App you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following data to operate the App:

  • User ID & Device ID — a randomly generated identifier and your device's vendor ID, used to save your progress and submit leaderboard scores.
  • Display name — the nickname you choose; shown publicly on the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay data — scores, reaction times, accuracy, streaks, achievements, and game mode history.
  • Purchase history — whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" option, processed via RevenueCat.
  • App interaction data — events such as game started, round completed, difficulty reached; used for analytics and improving the App.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, location, photos, or contacts.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect device-identifying information:

  • Supabase — stores user profiles, game sessions, and leaderboard data. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases and manages purchase entitlements. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. May use device identifiers to measure ad effectiveness. Privacy Policy
  • Amplitude — collects anonymized gameplay analytics events. Privacy Policy

Advertising & Tracking

If you have not purchased "Remove Ads," the App displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) to show relevant ads and measure their effectiveness. You can opt out at any time through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt or by going to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your device. Purchasing "Remove Ads" disables all advertisements in the App.

Leaderboard & Public Data

Your display name and scores are stored on a public leaderboard visible to all players. If you wish to change or remove your display name, you can do so in the App's Settings screen.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Supabase with row-level security policies that prevent unauthorized access.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing lefttospendapp@gmail.com with your User ID (found in Settings → App section). We will process your request and complete deletion within 30 days. Note that anonymized aggregate analytics data may be retained as it cannot be tied back to you individually.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
