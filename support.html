Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchases inside the app. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

My streak was lost — can it be restored?

Streaks are calculated based on daily activity. If you believe your streak was lost due to a technical issue, contact us at the email above and we will look into it.

How do I remove ads?

Tap Remove Ads on the main screen or in Settings. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all ads.

How do I delete my account?

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, send an email to lefttospendapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my account". We will process your request within 30 days.
